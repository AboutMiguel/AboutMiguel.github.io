The ATM program is a program offerred at Bancroft Middle School (my middle school). It stands for Advanced Technology Magnet and is a three year program.It is incredibly beneficial for students for a variety of reasons. In the first year it helps with all other classes as it teaches you all basic and advanced word processing as well as typing. In further years it delves deeper into useful and intruiging programs that preps students for a career in technology or just in basic technology skills. Due to the fact that very few students are accepted to this program, I am very greatful as well as lucky, to have been selected.
